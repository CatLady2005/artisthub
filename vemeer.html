Johannes Vemeer

Johannes Vemeer was a Dutch Baroque Period painter who specialized
in domestic interior scenes of middle-class life. During his lifetime,
he was a moderately successful provincial genre painter,
recognized in Delft and The Hague.

Although only about 36 of his paintings survive,
these rare works are among the greatest treasures in the world’s finest museums.
Vermeer began his career in the early 1650s.

Born: October 1632, Delft, Netherlands
Died: December 1675, Delft, Netherlands
