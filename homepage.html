My Favourite Artists

Hokusai

Hokusai was born in Edo, modern-day Tokyo, in 1760.
At 18 he entered the Art Studio
of Katsukawa Shunsho. He is best known for his book
'36 Views of Mount Fuji' and the most
famous print 'The Great Wave'


Michelangelo

Michelangelo di Lodovico Buonarroti Simoni, known simply as Michelangelo,
was an Italian sculptor, painter, architect and poet of the High Renaissance
was born in the Republic of Florence, who exerted an unparalleled influence
on the development of Western art.


Raja Ravi Varma

Raja Ravi Varma was an Indian painter and artist. He is considered among the greatest painters in the history of Indian art.
His works are one of the best examples of the fusion of European academic art
with a purely Indian sensibility and iconography.
